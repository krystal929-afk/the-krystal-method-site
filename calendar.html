System 1

The CRM Workflow

Track leads and clients by their status. Use color-coding and event titles to manage your pipeline without leaving the calendar.

Log a Lead: Create an "All-Day Event" on their follow-up date.
Event Title Format: [Status] Client Name (e.g., [AUDIT] Sarah, [DISCOVERY] Marcus, [INTENSIVE] Alex)
In the Description: Paste their Instagram link, email, and key notes about the project.
Color Code: Use calendar colors for status (Red = Hot Lead, Yellow = Warm, Green = Cold, Blue = Past Client)
System 2

The Social Workflow

Plan and automate your content directly from the calendar. Every post idea becomes a calendar event that triggers automation.

Create Content Events: Make a 15-minute event for each post idea.
Event Title Format: [POST] Caption or idea (e.g., [POST] Behind-the-scenes studio setup)
Automation via Make.com: When Make.com sees a "[POST]" event, it automatically posts to Instagram and Facebook at the scheduled time.
